About Us







Cavalieri Football Club started before pandemic occur year 2019, alumni of football community in letran are the founders of Cavalieri football club, with the support of our Club President Villie Lawas. Cavalieri Football Club is a place where young aspiring footballers can turn their dreams into reality. With a rich history of nurturing talent and fostering a deep love for the beautiful game, it's the perfect destination for kids who are passionate about playing football. The club not only provides top-notch coaching but also emphasizes the values of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. So, for all the young talents eager to kick-start their football journey, Cavalieri Football Club is where you can learn, grow, and, most importantly, have a blast on the pitch! Join us, lace up your boots, and let's score goals, make memories, and have a ball together!
